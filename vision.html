Origin

Aerosoft began as a small passion project between a handful of designers and engineers who missed the optimism of early consumer technology. The era when software felt like a window, not a wall, and devices were designed to delight. Frustrated by the growing coldness of modern interfaces, they set out to revive that spirit: building tools that felt warm, open, and genuinely joyful to use.

Current

Today, Aerosoft is a growing independent brand releasing both digital and physical products shaped by a single aesthetic and philosophy. Our small but dedicated team continues to work closely together, keeping our product line intentionally focused, prioritising depth of experience over breadth of features. Every release is shaped by our community and refined until it feels exactly right.

Future / Vision

Our vision is a world where everyday technology feels as natural as the air around it. Seamless, unobtrusive, and quietly beautiful. We're expanding our ecosystem with new tools, new devices, and new ways to connect the physical and digital, all under the same open-sky philosophy. The goal has never changed: make things worth caring about.
