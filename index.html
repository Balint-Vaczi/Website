Aero Music Player 2.0 — Now Available

March 15, 2026

We are thrilled to announce the release of Aero Music Player 2.0 — the most significant update to our flagship audio software since its original launch. Redesigned from the ground up with our community’s feedback at the core, version 2.0 brings a new level of clarity and immersion to your music listening experience.

The new release introduces an entirely reworked audio engine that delivers bit-perfect playback for lossless formats including FLAC, ALAC, and DSD. The visualizer has been overhauled with smooth 60 fps animated waveforms that respond dynamically to every frequency. The interface retains the glass-like translucency that defined the original, while adding a new adaptive colour palette that shifts subtly based on album artwork.

Aero Music Player 2.0 is available now as a free update for all existing users and as a free download for newcomers. Visit our Products page for full details, or read the independent review at The Verge ↗.
